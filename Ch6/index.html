Planets

There are eight planets in our solar system. They are:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

Stars

Stars are luminous spheres of plasma held together by gravity. The nearest star to Earth is the Sun.

Galaxies

A galaxy is a gravitationally bound system of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas, dust, dark matter, and other astronomical objects.
